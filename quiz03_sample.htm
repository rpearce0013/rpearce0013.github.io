Salisbury Geography Bee

Who we are

The Salisbury Geography Bee provides local residents with a competitive but fun experience,
testing their geography knowledge against their neighbors!

Participants compete based on their age, with adult, high school, middle school and
elementary school divisions.

Why participate?

  • Show what you know!
  • Interact with others who enjoy geography!
  • Win cool prizes!
  • Free to participate!
